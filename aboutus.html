About Us

What is BloodLink?

BloodLink is a web application that will oversee the management and collection o records of blood donors. This web application aims to maintain records of blood donors and help to track and search for the right donor with specific or certain blood type easily. It also aims to respond to the needs of the peopleby streamlining the process of contacting registered blood donors and enhancing communication.


This project will cater for the people who are willing to donate blood. It will let people register to the system so that the needed information for blood donation as well as their contact information can be recorded. As a result, blood donors can be easily reached and informed if they are free to donate especially during bloodletting events and in cases of emergencies.
