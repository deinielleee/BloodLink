Why should I donate?

Saves Lives

Blood donation saves lives. Blood transfusions are necessary for individuals who have lost blood due to injury, surgery, or medical conditions such as cancer or anemia.

Vital Source

Blood donation provides a vital resource for hospitals and medical facilities to treat patients in need of blood transfusions.

Improves Health

Blood donation improves health. Regular blood donation can have health benefits for the donor, including reducing the risk of heart disease, stroke, and certain cancers.

Maintain Iron Levels

Donating blood can also help maintain healthy iron levels in the body.
